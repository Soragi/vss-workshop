## Description: <br>
Use to summarize a recorded video via the LVS summarization microservice (HITL-gated) with a VLM fallback. Not for live RTSP captioning or incident-range reports. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Developers and engineers use this skill to generate narrative summaries of recorded video clips via the NVIDIA Video Search and Summarization microservice or a VLM fallback, enabling automated video understanding for monitoring and analytics workflows. <br>
